Career Site for Ball State University Career Center

Lead Designer

The KEY Careers website was made by the Digital Corps for freshman at Ball State University. The site helps undecided freshman find their career interests and explore all majors Ball State offers. KEY Careers concludes with a fun activity where students make an 'Action Plan' based on activities the Career Center offers and activities they create.

When designing the site, it was Rachel's and my goal to make the site as interactive and engaging as possible. Along with the website, I was the Lead Designer on the Admin panel, and worked closely with Lead Developer Josh Schoen. I learned a little Angular JS in the process of making the Admin Panel. Through analytics, we found that 25% of the entire freshman class went through the KEY Careers site, which was the largest response the Career Center had gotten back on past sites similar to KEY Careers.
