Career Site for Ball State University Career Center

Lead Designer

Through the Digital Corps, the KEY Careers website was made for freshman at Ball State University. The site helps undecided freshman find their career interests and explore all majors Ball State offers. KEY Careers concludes with a fun activity where students make an 'Action Plan' based on activities the Career Center offers and activities they create.

Our goal was to make the site as interactive and engaging as possible. Along with the website, I was the Lead Designer on the Admin panel, and worked closely with Lead Developer Josh Schoen. I learned some Angular JS in the process of making the Admin Panel. Through analytics, we found that 25% of the entire freshman class participated in KEY Careers site, which was the largest response the Career Center has gotten.
