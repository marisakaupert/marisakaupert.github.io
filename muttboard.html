Multiplayer Touch Board Launcher and Games

Lead Developer

Outside the Digital Corps on Ball State University's campus, there is a multi-touch board where people can play games. Originally, the board displayed one game at a time. It was my task to build a launcher where users could choose each game and return to the launcher where it had a variety of custom messages and showed the weather.

My first task was the build a bubble game designed by Aaram Rosco Kamali, named Charlie Destroys Worlds. After that was built, I then built the game 'Spot the Difference' which was designed by Emily Mixter and Ian Buchanan. Using the lancher designed by Aaram, I worked closely with Developer Lucas Smith to make sure each game worked on the board and that all of the older games were playable.

'Charlie Destroyer of Worlds' and 'Spot the Difference' can both be played on the board outside of the Digital Corps.
